Mian Muhammad Shehbaz Sharif

  • 2023 - present

    Shehbaz Sharif has served as Prime Minister since April 2022. A seasoned politician and member of the Pakistan Muslim League (N), he previously served as the Chief Minister of Punjab several times. His leadership is characterized by a focus on governance, economic recovery, and infrastructure development. He aims to stabilize the economy and improve public services. Facing challenges from political opponents and economic pressures, Shehbaz's tenure has been marked by efforts to foster unity and address pressing national issues.
